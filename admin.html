How to use Participants App

  1. Create an event — click "+ New event", fill in the event details including number of days and an optional MEL question.
  2. Share the registration link — copy the link from the event card and share it with participants before the event. They register, sign, and receive a unique code.
  3. On event day — open the registration form from the participant view to allow walk-ins to register on the spot.
  4. View & export — click "View participants" on any event to see who registered, search by name or code, and export to CSV.
